FURTHER INFORMATION

Groups

Take advantage of the RFEF advantages to book tickets exclusively and in advance. 

Supporters clubs and sports associations that wish to purchase tickets should send their request via email to grupotickets.rfef.es, specifying UWCL GROUPS in the subject line.

 

  • Only orders for more than 30 tickets will be processed subject to review.
  • This order is not a reservation and you will have to await a reply to your email in order to purchase your tickets.

PRM (Persons with reduced mobility)

These tickets include a ticket for a person with reduced mobility and a free ticket for the person accompanying them. 

To purchase tickets for users with reduced mobility, please send an e-mail to [email protected] specifying PRM and the match in the subject line and indicating, in the body of the message, the PRM zone/price you wish to purchase.
